The Vintage Camping Experience!

We are a camp ground committed to giving you the nostalgic feeling of the 1970s. With vintage caravans and

the lack of technology, you will feel transported back to nature and the 70s.



Thank you for visiting our website! Here, you can book a caravan, come to a party, or hike our trails!

We have a wide variety of retro caravans and even a few RVs. We also welcome tent campers!We even

provide vintage luxury tents for our tent campers. Here we welcome every type of camper and can not

wait to see you! Book your stay because the sooner we see you, the better!
